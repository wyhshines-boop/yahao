# Why AI Translation is Better for E-commerce

Traditional translation services are slow, expensive, and often miss the nuances of e-commerce content. Here's why AI translation is the future.

## Speed and Efficiency

AI translation can process thousands of words in seconds, compared to days or weeks with human translators. This speed is crucial for stores with large catalogs.

## Context Understanding

Modern AI models understand e-commerce terminology and can differentiate between "tank top" (clothing) and "tank" (container) based on context.

## Cost-Effective Scaling

As your store grows, AI translation scales with you without proportionally increasing costs.

## Consistent Quality

AI provides consistent translations across your entire product catalog, maintaining brand voice throughout.
