# Expanding to European Markets: A Guide

Europe represents a massive opportunity for Shopify store owners. With over 450 million consumers, the European market is diverse and lucrative.

## Understanding EU Languages

The EU has 24 official languages. Focus on key markets first:
- **German** - Largest economy in Europe
- **French** - Second largest economy
- **Spanish** - Fast-growing e-commerce market
- **Italian** - Strong fashion and design market

## Legal Requirements

EU regulations require product information to be in the local language. This isn't optional—it's the law.

## Payment and Shipping

Europeans prefer local payment methods. Consider integrating options like Klarna, iDEAL, and Bancontact.

## Cultural Considerations

Each European country has unique shopping habits. Germans value detailed product descriptions, while French customers appreciate elegant presentation.
